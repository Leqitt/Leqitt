About Our School

Caringbah High School is a co-educational, academically selective secondary school of around 910 students in the southern suburbs of Sydney. Each year in Year 7, students come from over fifty primary schools in the south-east metropolitan area. Caringbah High School has a tradition of educational excellence and our professional and skilled teachers are the key to that excellence.
